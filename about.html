Academic background, professional experience and scholarly profile.

Biography

Dr. Mohammad Abu Kausar is an Associate Professor and College Academic Advising Officer in the Department of Information Systems at the University of Nizwa, Oman. His work combines teaching, research, academic leadership and technology development. His recent research increasingly focuses on AI, digital twins, federated learning, IoT, cybersecurity and intelligent information systems.

Education

Ph.D. in Computer Science — Jaipur National University, India, 2016. Thesis: Design of Web Crawler for the Client-Server Technology: An Incremental Approach.
MBA (IT) — Symbiosis, Pune, India, 2012.
Master of Computer Science — G. B. Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, India, 2006.
Bachelor of Computer Applications — T.M. University, Bhagalpur, India, 2002.
